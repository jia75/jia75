docm

The goal of the docm command line utility is to allow users to have an organized collection of documents. This means: document tagging, rules about documents, document grouping, and probably more. To figure out what else could or should be included it is important or rather useful to imagine a use scenario.

Such a scenario could resemble an organization trying to collect its documentation on a specific project. The goal of docm is to facilitate the usage of the documentation collection, and to also facilitate the creation and addition of new documentation.

docm recommends a workflow with stages: for example, a new piece of documentation could go from a draft, to a working paper, to a deliberated document and finally get accepted and be active.
